Karachi City of Lights

Pakistan's largest city and main port, alive day and night.

Clifton Beach

Clifton Beach is one of the city's most visited public spaces, drawing families and vendors along its long, sandy shoreline. Evenings are especially lively, with camel rides and street food stalls lining the promenade.

Mohatta Palace

Built in 1927 as a summer residence, Mohatta Palace blends Mughal, Rajasthani, and European architectural styles using locally sourced pink stone. It now functions as a museum hosting art and cultural exhibitions.

Pakistan Maritime Museum

The Maritime Museum displays decommissioned naval vessels, submarines, and aircraft alongside exhibits covering Pakistan's coastal defence history, offering an unusual hands-on experience for visitors of all ages.
