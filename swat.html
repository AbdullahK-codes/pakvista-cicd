Swat — Switzerland of Pakistan

Alpine meadows, rivers, and a deep cultural history.

Malam Jabba

Malam Jabba, home to Pakistan's main ski resort.

Set at an elevation of around 2,800 metres, Malam Jabba is best known for its ski slopes and chairlift, drawing winter sports enthusiasts as well as summer visitors who come for the cool weather and pine-forest views.

Mingora Bazaar

Mingora Bazaar, the commercial heart of the Swat district.

Mingora is the largest town in Swat and its main trading hub. Its bazaars are known for local handicrafts, embroidered textiles, and gemstones sourced from the surrounding mountains.

Swat Museum

Swat Museum, housing Gandhara-era Buddhist artefacts.

The Swat Museum preserves artefacts from the region's Buddhist past, including sculptures and relics from the ancient Gandhara civilization that once flourished throughout the valley.
