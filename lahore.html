Lahore — City of Gardens

Pakistan's cultural capital, rich in Mughal history and warm hospitality.

Badshahi Mosque

Commissioned in the late 17th century, the Badshahi Mosque is known for its vast red-sandstone courtyard and intricate marble inlay work. It remains an active place of worship and one of the most photographed landmarks in the city.

Lahore Fort

Lahore Fort showcases centuries of architectural layering, from Akbar-era structures to later additions by Mughal and Sikh rulers. Highlights include the Sheesh Mahal (Palace of Mirrors) and the elaborately tiled Picture Wall along its northern face.

Shalimar Gardens

Laid out on three descending terraces, Shalimar Gardens were designed as a formal pleasure garden with hundreds of fountains and a sophisticated water-supply system, reflecting the Mughal ideal of paradise on earth.
