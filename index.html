Explore Pakistan

Pakistan is a land of striking contrasts — towering Himalayan and Karakoram peaks in the north, vibrant historic cities in the Punjab plains, and a bustling coastline along the Arabian Sea in the south. From Mughal-era forts and gardens to glacier-fed lakes and modern port cities, the country offers an extraordinary range of experiences for every kind of traveller. PakVista is a small guide to five of the country's most loved destinations, built to give visitors a quick, reliable overview before they plan a trip.

Featured Destinations

Lahore — City of Gardens

Mughal architecture, historic forts, and lush gardens.

Hunza Valley

Snow-capped peaks, turquoise lakes, and centuries-old forts.

Karachi — City of Lights

Beaches, colonial-era palaces, and maritime heritage.

Swat — Switzerland of Pakistan

Alpine valleys, ski slopes, and ancient Buddhist heritage.
